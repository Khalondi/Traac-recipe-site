  • Omelet

    • For this one you're going to need;

    1. eggs
    2. milk
    3. olive oil
    4. garlic
    5. paprika
    6. salt
    7. onions
    8. tomatoes

    Steps for preparation.

    • Mix up all your ingredients and fry on a hot pan for about 3 minutes.
    • Serve hot with mayonnaise and a cold glass of freshly squized juice.
  • Caramel

    • Caramel is a bit tricky to make because one wrong move could spoil this sweet delicacy. Here's what you're going to need to make it.

    1. sugar
    2. salt
    3. vanilla flavour
    4. dark chocolate
    5. very little water

    Steps for preparation.

    • Melt your sugar in on a preheated pan.When the sugar is completely melted, add your salt, chocolate and vanilla flavour.
    • You won't be needing the water unless the consistency of you caramel is too thick.
  • Chocolate Cake

    • Chocolate cake is one of my favourites to make. Here's all you need to make perfect cake.

    1. flour
    2. eggs
    3. chocolate
    4. milk
    5. sugar
    6. baking powder
    7. butter

    Steps for preparation.

    • Sift your flour into a nice big pan. then, and adequate spoons of baking powder.
    • For the wet ingredients, use a separate pan. Beat your butter and sugar until cream. After, add your eggs and melted chocolate along with vanilla flavour.
    • Finally, mix every thing together, put in a baking pan and bake in a preheated oven.
